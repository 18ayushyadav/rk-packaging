About RK Packaging

Quality packaging solutions from the heart of Delhi

We Build Packaging That Protects & Impresses

RK Packaging is a leading packaging solutions provider based in Naraina, Delhi. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality corrugated boxes, custom printed packaging, and hardboard boxes for businesses across India.

With a commitment to quality, durability, and customization, we serve industries ranging from electronics and FMCG to e-commerce and luxury goods. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility ensures every box meets the highest standards.

We believe that great packaging does more than protect — it communicates your brand's values and creates memorable unboxing experiences for your customers.
